Acme Corp Employee Benefits

Welcome to Acme Corp! This document outlines the benefits available to full-time employees.

Our comprehensive health insurance is provided by MegaHealth Inc. and includes medical, dental, and vision coverage. The plan year runs from January 1st to December 31st.

Paid Time Off (PTO): All full-time employees accrue 15 days of PTO per year. Accrual occurs monthly.

Retirement Plan: We offer a 401(k) plan with a company match. Acme Corp matches 50% of your contributions, up to 6% of your annual salary.

To enroll in these benefits or for any questions, please contact the HR department at hr@acme-corp.com or extension 450.
