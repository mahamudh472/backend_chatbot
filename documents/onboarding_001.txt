New Hire Onboarding Checklist - Week 1

Welcome to the team! Your first week will include:

Day 1:
- Meet your manager and team (9:00 AM)
- IT Setup: Receive your laptop and login credentials
- Complete HR paperwork (benefits, tax forms)

Day 2:
- Training: Company history and values
- Set up your development environment (for technical roles)
- Review your first project brief

Day 3:
- Training: Internal tools overview (Slack, Jira, Google Workspace)
- One-on-one with your manager to set goals

Day 4-5:
- Begin working on your first assigned tasks.
- Schedule coffee chats with your team members.

Your main point of contact is your assigned onboarding buddy, Sarah Lee (sarah.lee@acme-corp.com).
