Global Travel & Expense Policy - Version 2.1

This policy applies to all employees undertaking company-related travel.

Booking: All travel must be booked through the corporate "TravelEzy" portal to ensure compliance and insurance coverage.

Flights: Economy class is mandatory for all flights under 6 hours. For flights exceeding 6 hours, business class booking is permitted.

Expenses:
- Meals: A maximum daily allowance of $75 USD (including tax and tip) is provided.
- Lodging: Employees should book mid-tier business hotels. Receipts are required for all lodging expenses.

Approval: All international travel requests require pre-approval from a department head at least 14 days in advance.
