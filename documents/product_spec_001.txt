SynthoGizmo X200 - Technical Specifications

The SynthoGizmo X200 is our flagship wearable device.

Connectivity: Bluetooth 5.2, Wi-Fi 802.11ac
Battery: 450mAh Lithium-Polymer battery. Charging time: 2 hours. Usage time: up to 24 hours.
Sensors: Accelerometer, Gyroscope, Heart Rate Monitor, SpO2 sensor.
Display: 1.3 inch AMOLED touchscreen.
Compatibility: iOS 14+ and Android 10+.
Materials: Aerospace-grade aluminum casing with a silicone strap.
