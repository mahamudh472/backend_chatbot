Support Ticket #ST-7842

Status: Resolved
Product: SynthoGizmo X200
Customer: Jane Smith
Date: 2023-10-25

Issue Description:
Customer reported that the device will not charge. The screen remains blank when connected to the provided USB-C cable.

Troubleshooting Steps:
1. Asked customer to try a different USB power adapter. - No change.
2. Asked customer to inspect the charging port for debris. - Port was clear.
3. Guided customer through a hard reset (hold power button for 15 seconds). - No change.

Resolution:
The issue was determined to be a faulty charging cable. A replacement cable was shipped to the customer via expedited mail. Customer confirmed the new cable resolved the issue.
